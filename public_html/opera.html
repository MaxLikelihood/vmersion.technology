Interactive Live Entertainment

Our platform provides an avenue for audience participation in live performances.

Individuals often find themselves looking to become more engaged while viewing shows. From sitting in the audience and watching a live show on stage to sitting at home watching a live show on television. This is a passive and often boring source of entertainment. Life is not a spectator sport. Individuals are looking for a fulfilling way to become active participants.

Whether the audience is in the theatre or sitting at home, watching a simulcast, they are able to interact with the charactersâ€™ setting in a meaningful way. For example, in an opera house setting, with the classic Tosca, audience members would be able to control torches (flickering, brightness, red/orange/yellow color) and gargoyles on the castle walls (facial expressions, eye coloring, arm movements). Additionally, reality TV is constantly looking for more ways to connect with their audience. Through our platform, audiences can influence the show setting. Individuals who are given access to our platform are able to interact with the environment and feel present. For example, brewing coffee for their favorite character on Big Brother.
