Care and Safety

Our product provides an interactive, live video feed which allows users to view and interact with a remote environment.

Many people find themselves worrying about the well being of those they care about (grandparents, parents, children, physically challenged individuals, etc.). They are particularly worried about those prone to falls and accidents who may not be able to get help in the event of an emergency. They are looking for a way to provide assistance as necessary, should it be needed, and to look after loved ones from afar.

In the case where there is concern over the wellbeing of individuals living on their own, users can step in to ensure the safety and care of an individual. For example, an adult worrying about his or her parent(s) who he/she does not see on a regular basis would be able to check to make sure their parent was okay. In the event of an accident, such as a fall, they would be able to get help, let their parent know that help was arriving, and turn on the lights to make it more comfortable and help potential aid. They would also be able to turn off the oven and other electronic devices, in the event that any were left on. This eases the worrying of the individuals in the position of caring for the older parent as well as ensuring better care and more immediate responses to the needs of the person requiring aid.
