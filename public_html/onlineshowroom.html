Interactive Online Showroom

By combining telepresence and Internet of Things, individuals can explore products that were previously unavailable online.

In a world which is increasingly turning to the internet as its source and information on products and services, business owners are looking for a way to stay relevant to their customers. As more and more individuals turn to online shopping for their needs, business owners are working to give potential customers a better understanding of their products and services. Through the presently existing online platform potential customers are unable to get a true understanding of the product completely. They are looking a convenient way to have the sensation of exploring a product as though it were their own.

Our technology provides a platform for shoppers to interact with the product and test out the features completely before they buy the product. For example, in the setting of a pet shop or animal shelter, people could access the store online and play with pets through remote control of light, sound, and electronic toys, such as laser pointers, and electronic care items, such as food dispensers. This will help them bond with the pet as they discover their personality and preferences. Additionally, our platform can be used in larger scale businesses, such as the automobile industry, to supplement showrooms. Potential customers would be able to interact with the vehicle of their choice and discover its features and style from anywhere in the world. Interactions could include opening/closing the doors and windows, having an inside view of the car, navigating across different camera angles, switching the car on, exploring the dashboard and music options, and popping the hood to get an inside look at the engine.
